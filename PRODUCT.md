# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
GPU owners (gamers, creative professionals) with idle hardware wanting passive income. Crypto-curious individuals new to mining who want an accessible entry point. Solana ecosystem participants interested in $SoPU token staking and yield.

## Product Purpose
Solara Processing Unit is a decentralized GPU resource rental marketplace on Solana connecting GPU owners with those who need compute power for Proof of Work mining. The platform and $SoPU token democratize crypto mining. Success looks like: visitors buying $SoPU on DEX, joining Telegram, and anticipating the dApp launch.

## Brand Personality
Accessible, transparent, empowering. Not elite; democratizing. Confident without being intimidating; technical without being exclusive.

## Anti-references
Complex mining rig aesthetics (too intimidating). Generic "SaaS startup green" landing pages. Neon-on-black maximalist crypto. Over-designed DeFi platforms built for insiders only.

## Design Principles
1. Simplicity is the product — complexity is the competition's problem.
2. Show the future, don't explain it — let visuals suggest possibility.
3. Democratize the aesthetic — feels like it belongs to everyone, not just whales.
4. Earn trust through clarity — transparency in copy and layout signals trustworthiness.
5. Solana speed, human pace — the tech is fast; the UX should never feel rushed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Reduced motion support required (background video, spinning animations). No specific known user needs beyond general web accessibility.
